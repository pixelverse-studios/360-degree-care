## 3. Target Audience

### Primary Audience: Adult Children (Ages 40-65)
- **Demographics:** Middle-aged adults with aging parents
- **Pain Points:** Overwhelmed by care decisions, guilt about not providing care themselves, fear of making wrong choice
- **Search Behavior:** Research-heavy, comparison shopping, looking for trust signals
- **Decision Timeline:** 1-4 weeks from initial search to contact
- **Key Questions:**
  - "How much does home care cost?"
  - "What's the difference between home care and assisted living?"
  - "How do I know if my parent needs care?"

### Secondary Audience: Seniors (Ages 65+)
- **Demographics:** Seniors living independently who recognize need for help
- **Pain Points:** Desire to age in place, fear of losing independence, budget concerns
- **Search Behavior:** Direct, solution-focused, may search on behalf of spouse
- **Decision Timeline:** May be more immediate if triggered by health event

### Audience Insights for Content
- Both audiences prefer local, trustworthy providers over national franchises
- Reviews and testimonials heavily influence decisions
- Cost transparency is valued (many competitors hide pricing)
- Bergen County's affluent demographics can afford premium "concierge" positioning

## 4. Geographic Strategy

### Strategy: Hyper-Local Town-First

```
WIN FIRST:   City Pages     → "companion care fort lee nj"
THEN WIN:    County Hubs    → "companion care bergen county nj"
FUTURE:      Service Hubs   → "companion care nj" (state-level)
```

### Phase 1: Bergen County Dominance (Current Focus)

**Priority Order:**
1. Fort Lee - Affluent demographic, high-value clients
2. Ridgewood - Home base, maximum local trust
3. Paramus - Quick win (position 18 already)
4. Hackensack - Position 28, large population
5. Teaneck - High population density
6. Fair Lawn - Underserved by competitors
7. Englewood - Low competition
8. Westwood - Low competition
9. River Vale - Very low competition, quick win

**URL Structure:**
```
/services/[service]                              → State level (NJ)
/services/[service]/bergen-county                → County level
/services/[service]/bergen-county/[city]         → City level (PRIMARY FOCUS)
```

### Phase 2: County Expansion (After Bergen Success)
Only expand after Bergen County cities are ranking:
- Monmouth County
- Passaic County
- Essex County
- Ocean County

- ~118 quality pages currently indexed
